Overview

An institutional-quality purpose-built student accommodation asset located immediately adjacent to a leading European university campus. The property benefits from exceptional demand fundamentals driven by a growing student population and constrained local supply.

The residence comprises 420 beds across studio, en-suite, and cluster flat configurations, with comprehensive amenities including a fitness centre, landscaped courtyard, communal study rooms, and 24-hour reception services.

A nomination agreement with the university secures occupancy for 60% of beds, while the remaining units are let directly to students at competitive rents. The asset has maintained occupancy above 97% since opening, underpinning a stable and growing income stream.
