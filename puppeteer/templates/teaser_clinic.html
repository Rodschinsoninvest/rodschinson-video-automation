Overview

A compelling opportunity to acquire a purpose-built medical clinic in a prime urban healthcare corridor. The facility benefits from state-of-the-art infrastructure designed to meet the evolving demands of modern healthcare delivery.

The property comprises 6,800 m² of clinical and administrative space across three floors, featuring surgical suites, diagnostic imaging rooms, consultation offices, and a ground-floor pharmacy. Building systems include medical-grade HVAC, backup power generation, and advanced fire suppression.

The tenant roster includes established healthcare operators on long-term leases, providing highly defensive and inflation-linked income. The healthcare real estate sector continues to demonstrate resilient demand fundamentals driven by demographic trends and increasing outpatient care volumes.
