Overview

A compelling investment opportunity to acquire a purpose-built premium fitness and wellness centre in a prime urban location. The property is single-let to a leading fitness operator on a long-term triple-net lease, providing secure and low-management income.

The facility spans 4,500 m² across two levels, featuring a main gym floor, group exercise studios, an indoor swimming pool, sauna and steam facilities, and a dedicated CrossFit zone. The building was constructed to institutional standards with reinforced floors, high ceilings, and state-of-the-art ventilation systems.

The health and fitness sector continues to benefit from powerful structural tailwinds, including growing health consciousness, corporate wellness programmes, and the premiumisation trend. The tenant has demonstrated strong trading performance with consistent membership growth and above-market revenue per member.
