Overview

An exceptional opportunity to acquire a trophy 5-star resort and spa in one of the Mediterranean's most sought-after coastal destinations. The property commands a premium beachfront position with panoramic sea views across 4.2 hectares of landscaped grounds.

The resort comprises 186 keys including 24 suites, complemented by a world-class spa, three restaurants, conference facilities, and extensive leisure amenities including an Olympic-length infinity pool. All guest accommodations were fully renovated to luxury standards in 2023.

Operating under an internationally recognised brand with a proven management platform, the resort benefits from strong distribution channels and brand loyalty. Revenue performance has demonstrated consistent growth, with RevPAR reaching all-time highs driven by robust leisure demand and expanding corporate retreats.
