Overview

An attractive opportunity to acquire a fully-let retail-medical property anchored by a leading national pharmacy operator. The property occupies a prominent high-street corner position in a densely populated urban catchment with strong daily footfall.

The ground floor comprises 1,200 m² of prime retail pharmacy space fitted to modern dispensary standards, while the upper floors provide 1,250 m² of medical consulting rooms leased to established healthcare practitioners. The building benefits from a recent facade renovation and upgraded building services.

The pharmacy sector in Belgium continues to benefit from favourable regulatory dynamics and ageing demographics, providing a defensive and inflation-protected income stream. The triple-net lease structure ensures minimal landlord obligations and predictable cash flow characteristics.
