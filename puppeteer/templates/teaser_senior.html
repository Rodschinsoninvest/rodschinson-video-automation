Overview

A premium senior living residence offering a blend of independent living, assisted living, and specialised care units within a purpose-built, architecturally sensitive development set in landscaped grounds.

The property comprises 148 residential units across two interconnected wings, supported by communal dining, wellness, and medical facilities. Extensive gardens and sheltered outdoor spaces provide a restorative environment for residents.

The asset is operated under a long-term triple-net lease by one of Europe's most established senior care operators, ensuring institutional-quality management and stable, inflation-protected income. Demographic trends indicate strong and growing demand within the local catchment area.
