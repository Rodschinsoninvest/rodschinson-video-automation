Overview

A compelling opportunity to acquire a modern multi-level parking facility strategically located in the city centre, directly serving the surrounding retail, office, and residential catchment area.

The structure comprises 850 parking spaces across four levels, including 60 dedicated EV charging bays. The facility operates under a 25-year municipal concession with 18 years remaining, providing secure and predictable cash flows.

Recent upgrades include automated number-plate recognition, real-time occupancy guidance, and LED lighting throughout. The reinforced structure is engineered to support the addition of two further levels, offering significant expansion potential.
