Overview

A premier high-street retail portfolio comprising 34 units across a contiguous stretch of the city's most established shopping district. The assets benefit from exceptional pedestrian footfall and proximity to major transport nodes.

The portfolio includes a balanced mix of national anchor tenants and premium local retailers across fashion, food & beverage, and lifestyle categories. Ground-floor retail is complemented by upper-floor office and residential uses providing diversified income streams.

Recent facade and common area refurbishments have enhanced the streetscape appeal. Several leases incorporate turnover-based rent mechanisms, offering upside participation as trading conditions improve. The planned expansion of the adjacent transport hub is expected to further increase footfall and rental values.
